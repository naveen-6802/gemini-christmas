Gemini Christmas AI Chatbot

Welcome to the festive edition of Google Gemini AI, developed by Naveen Soni. This unique interactive chatbot brings the holiday spirit to your screen.

About Gemini Christmas

Unlike the standard Google Gemini, this version is purely designed for holiday cheer. Enter any prompt, and receive a warm Merry Christmas wish accompanied by a beautiful digital snow animation.

Features

  • Retro 8-bit aesthetic and design.
  • Minimal clean interface.
  • Dark and Light themes for comfortable viewing.
  • Instant holiday greetings.

Built using HTML, CSS, and pure JavaScript. No backend required.
